Who We Are

Star Container Lines (SCL) is a global logistics and freight forwarding company committed to simplifying international trade. With a presence across major shipping routes, we specialize in NVOCC, container trading, and end-to-end freight solutions for businesses worldwide.

Our team ensures that every shipment — whether by air, sea, or land — is handled with precision, reliability, and care. At SCL, we move your business forward, one container at a time.
